## Amethyst Chasm
![amethyst chasm](/assets/amethyst_chasm.png "amethyst chasm")
A structure found underground, around Y level -16 until bedrock.
This structure looks like a bigger version of the Amethyst Geode, and contains the [Scarlet Heart.](ITEMS.md#-scarlet-heart)

### Scarlet Core
The Scarlet Core is the obsidian sphere in the center of the structure.
Inside the sphere is a barrel containing the [Scarlet Heart.](ITEMS.md#-scarlet-heart)
When you open the barrel, 12 Scarlet Sentinels will spawn around the Amethyst Chasm and you will
have to kill them.
